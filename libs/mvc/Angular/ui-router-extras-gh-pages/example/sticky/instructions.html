Click the "Personnel" tab.
  • Drill down through the Personnel states until you are editing an employee record.
  • Modify some data on the employee record. There is intentionally no save button.
  • Click the "Inventory" tab and drill down the tab's states until you are viewing a Product.
  • Click the "Personnel tab". Deep State Redirect sends you back to the employee record state, while Sticky State preserves the DOM and $scope exactly as you left it (including scroll position, etc).
  • Click the "Inventory" tab. Deep State Redirect sends you back to the product record, and Sticky State preserves the DOM and $scope.

Each sticky state has its own named ui-view. This demo hides the preserved DOM for inactivated states using the ng-show directive. The DOM for inactivated states can be viewed using your browsers dev tools. 

<div ui-view="peopletab" ng-show="$state.includes('top.people')" class="col-sm-8"></div>
<div ui-view="invtab"    ng-show="$state.includes('top.inv')"    class="col-sm-8"></div>
<div ui-view="custtab"   ng-show="$state.includes('top.cust')"   class="col-sm-8"></div>
